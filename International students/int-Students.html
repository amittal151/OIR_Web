International Students

Welcome to IIT Kanpur!
IITK welcomes you on your new journey to study, explore and learn. Foreign nationals can either apply for Degree Programs (Master/Doctoral) or Non-Degree Programs (Course work / Research not leading to a degree) here. Admission into IIT Kanpur's undergraduate programs is conducted by the Joint Admission Board.
