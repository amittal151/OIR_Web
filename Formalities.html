Formalities after Arrival

Fill and submit the following forms at the IR Office, once you arrive in IITK:

  • Joining Form (as soon as possible after arrival)
  • Health Fitness Form (required only if you do not have health insurance)
  • Residential Permit Form (the IR office will work on this with you)
